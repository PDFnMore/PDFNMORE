Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I convert a PowerPoint to PDF?

Converting your presentation to PDF ensures that everyone sees your slides exactly as you designed them, with all fonts, images, and formatting intact, regardless of the device or software they use.

Will my animations and transitions be preserved?

No. PDF is a static format, so animations, transitions, and embedded videos from your PowerPoint will not be included in the final PDF. Each slide will be converted into a static page.

Are my presentation files secure?

We prioritize your privacy. Files are sent securely to the Cloudmersive conversion API and are not stored long-term. For highly sensitive documents, always consider the privacy policy of any online service.
