Online Image Rotator & Flipper

Rotate images to any angle, 90° clockwise/counter-clockwise, or flip them horizontally and vertically with simple controls.

0°

How to Rotate or Flip an Image

  1. Upload an Image: Drag your image file onto the upload area, or click to select a file.
  2. Transform Your Image: Once the image appears, use the controls:
    • Use the slider for fine-tuned rotation to any angle.
    • Click the "Left 90°" or "Right 90°" buttons for quick turns.
    • Use the flip buttons to mirror the image horizontally or vertically.
  3. Reset View: If you make a mistake, click "Reset View" to revert all rotations and flips without re-uploading the image.
  4. Download the Result: When you're happy with the result, click "Download Image" to save it. To use a different image, click "Start Over".

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool secure to use with my personal photos?

Absolutely. This tool operates 100% within your browser. Your images are never sent to any server, ensuring your files are completely private and secure.

Does rotating or flipping an image affect its quality?

No. The transformations performed by this tool are 'lossless', meaning they do not re-compress the image data in a way that degrades its quality. The resulting image will have the same clarity as the original.

What file formats can I use with this tool?

Our rotator and flipper tool works with all common image formats, including JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, and BMP. The downloaded image will be saved in the same format as the one you uploaded.
