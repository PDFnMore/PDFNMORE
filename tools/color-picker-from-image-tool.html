Online Color Picker from Image

Upload an image and use our magnifying glass to pick the perfect color. Instantly get HEX and RGB codes for your design projects.

Move your mouse over the image to see colors. Click to select.

How to Pick a Color from an Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag an image file onto the designated area or click to select one from your device.
  2. Hover to Find Colors: Move your mouse cursor over the uploaded image. A magnifying loupe will appear, showing you a zoomed-in view of the pixels.
  3. See Live Color Values: As you move the mouse, the color swatch and the HEX/RGB code boxes will update in real-time with the color of the pixel directly under your cursor.
  4. Click to Select: When you find the perfect color, simply click the mouse. This will "lock" the color values in the display boxes.
  5. Copy the Code: Click the copy button next to the HEX or RGB code to copy it to your clipboard for use in your projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images safe with this tool?

Yes, 100% safe. This tool is powered by client-side JavaScript, which means your image is processed directly in your browser. It is never uploaded to our or any other server, ensuring your privacy.

What's the difference between HEX and RGB?

Both are ways to represent colors digitally. RGB (Red, Green, Blue) uses three numbers (from 0 to 255) to define the intensity of red, green, and blue. HEX (Hexadecimal) is a six-digit code that represents the same RGB values in a more compact format, commonly used in web design (CSS).

How accurate is the color picker?

It is pixel-perfect. The tool reads the exact color data from the single pixel directly underneath your mouse cursor, providing the most accurate color values possible from the image.
