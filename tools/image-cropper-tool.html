How to Crop an Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Start by dragging your image file into the upload box, or click it to select a file from your computer.
  2. Adjust the Crop Box: Once your image appears, a cropping overlay will be visible. Click and drag the box to move it, and use the corner and side handles to resize the selection area.
  3. Confirm and Crop: When you are happy with the selected area, click the "Crop Image" button.
  4. Download Your Image: A preview of your newly cropped image will be shown. Click the "Download" button to save it to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image cropping tool secure?

Yes, completely. All processing is done locally in your web browser. Your original image is never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your files remain private and secure.

Does cropping an image reduce its quality?

No, cropping does not reduce the quality of the selected area. It simply discards the parts of the image outside your selection. The pixels inside the crop box remain unchanged, so the resolution of the cropped portion stays the same as the original.

Can I set a specific aspect ratio for my crop?

This version of the tool offers free-form cropping. For precise aspect ratios (like 16:9 for thumbnails or 1:1 for profile pictures), you can use our Image Resizer tool after cropping to get the exact dimensions you need.

What file formats can I crop?

Our tool works best with the most common web image formats: JPG/JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can upload and crop these files with ease.
