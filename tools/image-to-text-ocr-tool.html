How to Convert Image to Text

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag your image file to the upload area or click to select it from your device.
  2. Select the Language: Choose the language present in your image from the dropdown list. This greatly improves accuracy.
  3. Extract Text: Click the "Extract Text" button. Please be patient, as the OCR process may take a moment. The progress bar will show the current status.
  4. Copy Your Text: Once completed, the extracted text will appear. Click the "Copy" button to copy it to your clipboard. To process a new image, click "Start Over".

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the text recognition?

The accuracy of our OCR tool is very high, but it depends heavily on the quality of the uploaded image. For best results, use images that are clear, well-lit, not blurry, and feature standard, easy-to-read fonts. Skewed or low-resolution images will result in lower accuracy.

Is it safe to upload my documents here?

Yes, your privacy is guaranteed. This tool uses Tesseract.js, which runs the entire OCR process inside your web browser. Your images are never sent to our or any third-party servers.

Why is the process taking a long time?

OCR is a computationally intensive task. The first time you run the tool for a specific language, your browser needs to download the language data file (usually 5-10 MB). Subsequent runs for the same language will be much faster. The progress bar will keep you updated on the status.

What languages are supported?

Our tool supports a wide range of languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and many more. Simply select the appropriate language from the dropdown menu before starting the extraction process for the best possible results.
